Founder Haley Stewart

Haley's passion started in her youth. She was very active with members of her school with disabilities. This exposure at an early age created her caring and empathetic personaility. After high school Haley completed her post secondary schooling at St.Clair College in Developmental Service Worker.

After obtaining her college diploma haley would go on to work for Victoria Manor in Windsor, Ont. This employment would give her a deeper understanding of the day to day operations of a live in care giving establishment. She would obtain more skills and traing, including RPN training.

Haley's dream finally came true when she got the funding to open up Sunshine Seekers and began spreading her positivity and training to the Greater Essex County region. She has continued to fight for disability rights, while also keeping up the the constant demands of Sunshine Seekers.
