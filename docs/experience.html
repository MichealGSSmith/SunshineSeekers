The Sunshine Seeker Experience!

Sports and Recreational Activities

Mental health and physical health have been shown to be linked together and here at Sunshine Seekers we fully embrace and support all our seekers to join and participate in a range of different local sport activites, from wheelchair basketball, all the way to bell ball. What ever physical activity interests you, we will find a way for you to participate!

Community and Inclusion

We believe that to really experience the joys of life, it starts with finding and planting some deep roots in the community. Thats why we activily particpate in community events, ranging from local concerts to charities!

Inter-Personal Skills

We understand what it is like when the world disregards you for your differences but we will teach the skills needed to navigate through the world while also recongizing the emotions everyone is feeling!
